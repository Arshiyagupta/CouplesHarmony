This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Marriage Enrichment app, designed to help couples achieve lasting love through tools, resources, and activities.

1.1 Personal Information:  
Name  
Email address  
Gender  
Date of birth  
Relationship status  
Responses to surveys, assessments, or questionnaires within the app

1.2 Usage Data:  
Device information (e.g., IP address, device type, operating system)  
Activity within the app (e.g., features used, time spent, interactions with content)

1.3 Sensitive Data (Optional):  
You may choose to share sensitive personal information, such as details about your relationship experiences, goals, or challenges. We will only collect and process such sensitive data with your explicit consent.

 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your personal data to provide and improve the Marriage Enrichment app experience, including:
Personalizing your app experience and offering tailored recommendations
Facilitating communication between couples
Enhancing features and developing new tools based on user feedback
Sending notifications, reminders, and updates about new content and features
Analyzing app usage to improve performance and ensure it meets user needs

 3. How We Protect Your Information
We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:
Data encryption
Secure server infrastructure
Access control policies
Regular security audits and updates
Despite these efforts, no system can guarantee complete security, and we cannot ensure the absolute security of your data.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:
Service providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate and maintain the app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics services). These vendors are bound by confidentiality agreements.
Legal purposes: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users, we may share information with law enforcement or regulatory bodies.
Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of the company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such changes.

 5. Your Privacy Choices
You have the right to:
Access, update, or delete your information: You can update your profile, change your preferences, or delete your account through the app settings. If you wish to delete all personal data associated with your account, please contact us.
Opt-out of communications: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails or push notifications at any time by using the unsubscribe option or adjusting your app settings.
Withdraw consent: If you have provided consent for sensitive data processing, you can withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us, and we will cease processing your sensitive data.

 6. Children's Privacy
The Marriage Enrichment app is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove that information.
